Welcome Unlimited FAQs

Learn about Welcome Unlimited, our mobile phone plan with unlimited data. Visit our Unlimited plans page to see pricing and switch today.

  • How do I get Welcome Unlimited right now?
  • What discounts are available with Welcome Unlimited?

About Welcome Unlimited

Expand All

  1. What is Welcome Unlimited?

    Welcome Unlimited is a monthly unlimited mobile phone plan. When you have Welcome Unlimited, any other phones on your account each must also have this plan.

    Here's what you get with Welcome Unlimited:

    • Unlimited voice, messaging and data TT
    • Mexico & Canada talk, text & data TT
    • Unlimited international texting from the US TT
    • 5G Nationwide TT, 4G LTE TT
    • Disney+ for 6 months TT
    • Apple Arcade TT or Google Play Pass TT for 6 months
    • Apple Music for 6 months TT
    • Verizon Up rewards
    • Call Filter spam blocker available at no charge TT

    Welcome Unlimited can't be mixed and matched with other Unlimited plans. Welcome Unlimited isn't eligible for device purchase promotions (e.g., Buy One Get One, trade-ins, etc.).

  2. What's the Welcome Unlimited plan's monthly cost?

    The cost of each Unlimited plan depends on how many mobile phone lines you have and if you sign up for the $10/line per month Auto Pay and paper-free billing discount. Visit our Unlimited plans page to get pricing for the number of lines you need.

    For example, with 4 or more lines and the Auto Pay and paper-free billing discount, each Welcome Unlimited line is $30/month.

  3. What other phone plans can I have on my account along with Welcome Unlimited?

    None. You can have up to 12 phone lines with Welcome Unlimited on your account. Welcome Unlimited can't be mixed and matched with other Unlimited plans. When you have Welcome Unlimited, any other phones on your account each must also have this plan.

    You can mix and match with 5G Start, 5G Play More, 5G Do More and/or 5G Get More.

    You can also mix and match with these plans if you already have them: Get More Unlimited, Do More Unlimited, Play More Unlimited, Above Unlimited, Beyond Unlimited, Go Unlimited. However, these plans can no longer be added to accounts.

  4. Do I have to have a 5G phone to use Welcome Unlimited?

    No. You can use your 4G device. However, you need a 5G-capable device to access our 5G networks.

    Learn more about our 5G Nationwide network and 5G devices. Or shop 5G devices.

  5. What discounts are available with Welcome Unlimited?

    With Welcome Unlimited, you may also be eligible for:

    • $10/line per month discount for using Auto Pay and paper-free billing. TT
    • Plan, feature and accessories discounts if you're a first responder, military member or veteran, nurse, teacher or college student.
    • Mobile + Home Rewards - Earn discounts when you pair a mobile phone plan and Fios Internet.

    Welcome Unlimited isn't eligible for device purchase promotions (e.g., buy one get one, upgrade deals, trade-ins, etc.). You can still bring your own device (BYOD) to Verizon with this plan.

Adding the plan to your account

Expand All

  1. How do I get Welcome Unlimited right now?

    Here's how to get Welcome Unlimited if you:

    • Are new to Verizon - Sign up on our Verizon Unlimited plans page today.
    • Have a different Verizon mobile plan - Add a line or switch your plan to Welcome Unlimited today. If you're switching to Welcome Unlimited from a shared data phone plan, all phone lines on your account must change to Welcome Unlimited plans.
